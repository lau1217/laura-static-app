By Crit McGillicutty

Over whale-med

The procrastinating pirate tried to rule the whole sea. She was a crew member of many ships. She thought this way she would for sure reach her dream of becoming the best pirate she could be. When one shipped docked, she would hop on the other. It took meticulous planning and timing to pull this off, but many times it didnâ€™t work.
